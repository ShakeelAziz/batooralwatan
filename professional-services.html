Detailed scope — all 19 services

1 Complete Authorities Approvals

End-to-end coordination for approval packages across relevant entities — structured checklist, document compilation, and status tracking until clearance.

2 DDA (Dubai Development Authority) Approvals

Support for DDA-governed developments: submission formats, technical queries, and revisions aligned to authority feedback.

3 Dubai Municipality (DM) Approvals

DM-related building and fit-out routes — drawings, inspections where applicable, and compliance with municipal requirements.

4 Trakhees Approvals

Coordination for projects under Trakhees — documentation, fee cycles, and liaison for design and execution approvals.

5 Nakheel Approvals

Nakheel community and master-developer requirements — submissions for modifications, additions, and contractor alignment.

6 DEWA Approvals

Electrical and water authority applications, load requests, and coordination for energization and service connections.

Authorities & documentation — NOCs, drawings, permits, and inspections across Dubai entities.

7 G+1 Villa Construction (Complete Turnkey Solutions)

End-to-end G+1 villa delivery — structure, envelope, and coordinated trades — scoped as a turnkey package with agreed milestones.

8 Service Block Construction

Construction of standalone utility/service blocks — structural shell, openings, and MEP rough-ins per design.

9 Maid Room Construction

New maid room additions or conversions — structural, waterproofing, and finishing aligned to approved layouts.

10 Partition Works (All Types)

Metal stud, gypsum, glass, and block partitions — supply, installation, and finishing for offices and residential units.

11 Authority Approval for Partitions & Modifications

Specific approvals for partition layouts and internal modifications — matching authority and developer conditions before work on site.

12 Civil Works

Concrete, blockwork, screeds, and structural touch-ups — integrated with following trades and inspection schedules.

13 MEP Works (Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing)

Routing, installation, testing, and commissioning support for HVAC, power, lighting, and plumbing systems.

Delivery & coordination — drawings, permits, site execution, and handover.

14 Drawing Preparation & Submission

Preparation or coordination of architectural, structural, and MEP drawings for submission — revision control and register of issued drawings.

15 Permit Approvals & Coordination with Authorities

Permit tracking, resubmissions, and multi-authority coordination so site work aligns with issued permits and validity windows.

16 Renovation & Modification Works

Attendance and follow-up for scheduled inspections, snag lists, and formal clearance milestones.

Site supervision, contractor coordination, and progress reporting against schedule and quality benchmarks.
